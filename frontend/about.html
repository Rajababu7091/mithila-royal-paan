Our Heritage & Farming

Cultivating luxury betel leaf varieties and presenting ancient hospitality traditions to the modern world.

Sourced Directly from Madhubani, Bihar

For generations, our family-owned farms in the Mithila region of Bihar have nurtured the delicate Magahi betel leaf. Our crop relies on clean, standard groundwater irrigation and fully organic composting methods.

Betel leaves (Paan) hold a central position in the culture of Mithilanchal. Combined with Makhana (foxnuts), it represents honor, digestive well-being, and respect. No guest leaves a Mithila home without receiving the traditional token of love.
